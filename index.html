Gamma Nu Eta

ITWS Honor Society

Gamma Nu Eta is the ITWS honor society here at rpi. Founded in 2000 GNH's connects ITWS students and RPI alumni. GNH hosts many events that help students with netwrking, and also offer tutoring every week where students are welcome to bring any questions they have on homework from ITWS related classes to even career related questions.
